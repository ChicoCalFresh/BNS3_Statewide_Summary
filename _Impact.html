Impact

The following questions were asked in regards to the impact of Basic Needs programs and services on students lives. Students were asked to evaluate how they feel these programs have benefited them both academically and non-academically, as well as barriers/reasons in regards to why they are not using certain programs. This includes programs such as CalFresh, Food Pantries, Medi-Cal (also known as Medi-Cade in the United States), and Emergency Housing Services.


CalFresh EBT

Note: for the first graph on EBT application we have (n=652, 31% of 2110 reporting) And for the second graph on how many people are fed we have (n=620, 29% of 2110 reporting)

Looking at the above graphs, the majority of people apply for CalFresh EBT as an individual (355 people) while only 297 people applied with other people in their household. Similarly, the majority of people claimed only 1 person is fed through the EBT card (230 people). 138 people claim to feed 2 people through the EBT card, and only a small amount of people claim to feed 5 or more people through the EBT card.


Monthly CalFresh Money

How much money do you receive from CalFresh each month? (n=634, 30% of 2110 reporting)

The average amount of money received per household using CalFresh (denoted by the dotted line) is $305.91, while the median is $250. The values range from $0 to $1500. The majority of households who do receive these benefits seems to be getting around $250 or less per month.

Is This Enough?

Note: for the first graph on if the allowance is enough we have (n=623, 30% of 2110 reporting) And for the second graph on when people run out we have (n=586, 28% of 2110 reporting)

Of the people who do receive money from CalFresh, 224 of them agree that it is enough money to provide healthy food for a month and 288 of them feel that it is not enough. People typically run out of CalFresh money in the first week (127 people), the second week (156 people) or the third week (160 people). A smaller minority run out within the fourth week or do not use up all their monthly balance.

Food Pantry Use

How often do you normally use the food pantry or if you are not currently using it, how often did you use it? (n=500, 24% of 2110 reporting)

When people use the food pantry, the majority do not use it every week (235 people). 180 people claim to use it about once a week, 27 people use it more than once a week, and 58 people claim other uses.
